About Me

CV


Charlotte is a mechanical engineer specializing in biomechanics and aerospace. She has experience in finite element analysis, mechanics, imaging, design, 3D bioprinting, material characterization, and project management. She is currently a mechanical enginering analysit at L3Harris. Previously, Charlotte was a Senior Scientist in brain biomechanics research and the associate director of the engineering design makerspace at Washington University and consulted for a neuroscience startup. Charlotte is excited to leverage her extensive analysis, research, design, and fabrication experience in R&D engineering.

Projects & Activities

Maker Task Force

Charlotte 3D printing face shields for Barnes Jewish Hospital at the onset of COVID-19 in 2020 as a part of WashU's Maker Task Force. Charlotte designed the WashU Shield based on feedback from doctors at BJC. Over 1000 face shields were fabricated using 3D printers and a laser cutter and delivered to the hospital, until materials were no longer available.

Read more about it here.

ENT Surgical Device

Ear nose throat surgical device, designed as a low-cost replacement for Da Vinci Robot in the performance of back-of-throat and skull base surgeries. The project was designed and fabricated at Yale University's makerspace in 2013.

Automated Wheelchair Storage Device

Prototype of automotive wheelchair storage device for manual wheelchair users. The device attaches to the roofracks of any vehicle and automatically moves wheelchair from driver's door to rear of car to improve manual wheelchair user's independence and prevent overuse injuries caused by self-storage. Charlotte led the team that designed and built the device through a student-led incubator, Sling Health. The project was a finalist for the Sling Health Demo Day in 2015 and won the Bio Entrepreneurship Core's Bench to Business Award in 2016. A patent was awarded in 2018. Charlotte also served as the COO (2017-2019) and Facilities Manager (2015-2017) for Sling Health.

Therapy Dog Volunteering

Charlotte and her rescue dog, Loki, at Loki's therapy dog graduation in 2019. Loki and Charlotte have volunteered as a therapy dog team at BJC Hospital and multiple universities in the greater St. Louis area.

Home Climbing Wall

Charlotte climbing on her backyard wall that she designed and built during the summer of 2020 when climbing gyms shut down due to COVID-19.
